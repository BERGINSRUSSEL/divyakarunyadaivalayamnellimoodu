Blessed Sacrament Church

Divyakarunya Daivalayam in other words Blessed Sacrament Church is a famous centre of pilgrimage, under the Latin Catholic Diocese of Neyyattinkara. People from all parts of Kerala and outside, irrespective of caste and creed come to the church seeking blessings. It is the first church in India to be a pilgrimage center of Blessed Sacrament . The Church was built in 1924.
The church is nearing it's Centenary celebrations. During its existance it has seen a whole lot of transformations ups and downs and so on.
