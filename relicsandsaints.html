Relics

Relic usually consists of the physical remains of a saint or the personal effects of the saint or venerated person preserved for purposes of veneration as a tangible memorial. Relics are an important aspect of some forms of Christianity. Relic derives from the Latin reliquiae, meaning "remains", and a form of the Latin verb relinquere, to "leave behind, or abandon".

Saints

A saint is a person who is recognized as having an exceptional degree of holiness or likeness or closeness to God. In christianity we seek the saints to pray to the lord for us. In our church many statues of saints are erected as statues an filial churches through whom we seek the blessing of Lord Jesus.
